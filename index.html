About

NUMBER is a real-time stage tracker for dance competition families. Know exactly what number is on stage, get community alerts about delays, and track your dancer's upcoming numbers — all in one place.

If you need help, have a question, or want to report an issue, you're in the right place. We typically respond within 24 hours.

What NUMBER Does
🎭
Live Stage Tracker
See what number is on stage right now, updated by the community in real time.
🔔
Community Alerts
Judge breaks, delays, order changes — reported and confirmed by other parents.
💜
My Numbers
Add your dancers and track exactly how far away each of their numbers is from stage.
🏆
Competitions
Join your event and access schedules and scoring docs shared by your team.
